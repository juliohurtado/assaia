# Test Assignment – Systems Analyst

This assignment aims to assess the candidate’s knowledge and skills in the following areas:

- **Process design**
- **Task formalization**
- **Ability to work with incomplete or missing input data**

The main objective of this assignment is to evaluate the candidate’s general ability to perform **system analysis**, define **clear requirements**, and design **robust operational processes**.

---

## Assignment

### 1. Passenger Journey Process Design

Design the process of going through the **main airport formalities** from the **passenger’s point of view**, based on your personal observations and reasonable hypotheses.

The process description should cover the **basic steps from entering the departure airport to boarding the plane**.

Your description should include:

- Participants involved in the process
- Inputs and outputs at each step
- Alternative scenarios and edge cases
- Mentions of possible system integrations

---

### 2. Automated Workstation Design

Imagine that you are working in the **IT department of an airport** and you need to create an **automated workstation** for **one of the steps** described in the process above (of your choice).

Normally, each workstation is equipped with a **PC connected to the airport’s network**.

Create a **sample task description** for developers who should implement the software for this automated workplace (for example, a **Jira ticket** or a similar work item).

---

### Notes

- You are free to use **any notation or style** you prefer.
- The focus is on **clarity, structure, and reasoning**, not on implementation details.
